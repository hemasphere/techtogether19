Breast Cancer

    Symptoms

    Note: Some people can have no symptoms but others may experience the following:
  • Discomfort
  • Inverted Nipple
  • Lumps
  • Nipple Discharge

Preventions

  • Limit Alcohol
  • Don't Smoke
  • Control Your Weight
  • Be Physically Active

    Cardiovascular Disease-Heart Disease

    Symptoms

  • Chest Pain
  • Chest Tightness
  • Shortness of Breath

Preventions

  • Don't Smoke or Use Tobacco
  • Exercise For About 30 Minutes on Most Days of the Week
  • Eat a Heart-Healthy Diet
  • Manage Stress
  • Get Regular Health Screenings

    Lung Cancer

    Symptoms

  • Chest Pain
  • Cough can be chronic, dry, severe or with blood
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Frequent Respiratory Infections

Preventions

  • Don't Smoke
  • Avoid Second Hand Smoke
  • Get Your Home Tested for Radon

    Diaphragm Disease

    Symptoms

  • Discomfort or Difficulty Breathing
  • Pain in the Chest, Shoulder or Abdominal Area
  • Hypoxemia (a lack of oxygen in the blood)

Preventions

  • Avoiding Foods That Cause Heartburn or Acid Reflux
  • Eating Smaller Portions
  • Quitting Smoking and Heavy Drinking
  • Improving Posture

If you are experiencing the above symptomps for a long time, please consider consulting a doctor near you.
